Hey there! I'm Adarasha Gaihre (Aakku) , an IT student currently pursuing my BSc in Computer Science and Information Technology (BSc.CSIT) in Nepal. I'm passionate about coding, anime (huge Naruto fan!), and working on creative web projects. I enjoy spending time with friends, drawing, and continuously learning new skills in the world of development.

I’ve been honing my coding skills for over six months now, with experience in C, C++, C#, and HTML/CSS. Currently, I’m diving deeper into Python and exploring new technologies like Swift and Rust to expand my toolkit. I have a strong interest in security, networking, and cloud computing, and I dream of becoming a software engineer one day.

I’m also fascinated by web development, both front-end and back-end, and have been working on some exciting projects, like this portfolio!
One of my proudest achievements so far has been leading my team, "The JEEZ," to second runner-up in the Nepathya Intra-College RC Robo Racing competition. The project involved designing and building an RC car from scratch, where we collaborated on everything from hardware assembly to software implementation. This experience deepened my understanding of robotics and teamwork, and I’m excited to continue working on such innovative projects in the future.

Whether it’s building websites, experimenting with animations, or collaborating on tech projects like JEEZ, I’m always eager to learn and apply my skills in creative ways.
